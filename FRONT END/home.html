ABOUT US

Inspired to make the world a better place a group of socially driven enthusiasts come together to create little joys and happiness by creating fundraisers aimed towards the bearers of tiny hearts.The monetory support obtained from the events helps us to make a sustainable impact on the lives of those little folks we come in touch with.....

Established in 2005, we have managed assemble the citizens of Bengaluru to a collective purpose that is to create and sustain the happiness among tiny humans.

If you would like to assist us in any way or know of a child who might need help, kindly contact us
